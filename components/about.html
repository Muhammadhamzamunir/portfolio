About Me

What I am all about.

A software engineer with a passion for turning challenging business needs into clean, scalable software. I'm a Full Stack Developer with a strong focus on the MERN stack, Laravel, and Vue.js—building web and mobile applications that solve real problems with an eye for performance and user experience.

From Hasilpur to Lahore, I've grown from early curiosity in tech to shipping production applications. I completed my BS in Software Engineering at COMSATS University Lahore, cut my teeth as a Frontend Intern with React and Laravel, and now build full-stack and mobile solutions. I'm always looking to improve, experiment with new tools, and work with teams that care about quality and clarity in code.

Whether it's REST APIs, responsive UIs, or cross-platform apps with React Native, I like to own the full picture—from idea to deployment—and keep the codebase maintainable and the UX smooth.
